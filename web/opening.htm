The laboratory focuses on unearthing scientific principles and providing solutions to key problems in Healthcare of Wearable Materials in four major areas: Traditional Chinese medical therapies and their materials, energy materials and healthcare, personal protective equipment against infectious diseases as well as spider silks and their relatives as biomaterials.

As such, we apply advanced methods including custom-made wearable electronics as basic tools to examine materials, their application methods, and particularly their interactions with human body in terms of physical, chemical, biological, physiological and informational relations. From the discoveries and models of basic research, applied investigation, product developments and standardization are envisaged, which can produce societal as well economic impact in addition to scientific advances.

Requirements

Applicants for the above posts should have Master's degree/ PhD in one of the following disciplines:

  • Microbiology/Molecular biology
  • Electrical and Electronic Engineering
  • Wearable Electronics
  • Quantum Physics/Photonics/Electromagnetism
  • Chinese Medicine/General Medicine
  • Mathematical/Physical/Structural Modelling/Simulation/Data Mining
  • Water Chemistry
  • Polymer/Fiber Science and Engineering/Textiles

English Proficiency Requirements of College of Science and Engineering:

  • a minimum TOEFL score of 550 (paper-based) or 213 (computer-based) or 79 (internet-based); or
  • a minimum overall band score of 6.5 in IELTS; or
  • other test scores that may be regarded as equivalent to TOEFL 550 (paper-based) or 213 (computer-based) or 79 (internet-based).
(Please note that individual Departments in the College may adopt a higher standard.)
