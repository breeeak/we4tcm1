Dr. HO Kai Chiu

Ph.D., MA, FHKITA, C.TEXT, FTI, SMCTES

kc.ho@hansktech.com

Biography

Dr. Ho has more than 40 years' work experience in Hong Kong's manufacturing industry, including innovative textiles and clothing technologies, innovation and technology strategy, R & D project design, funding support, management and commercialization, etc. He is also a Chartered Textile Technologist and a Fellow Member of the Textile Institute; Fellow Member of Hong Kong Institute of Textile and Apparel; Senior Member of China Textile Engineering Society, etc.

Dr. Ho is currently in charge of Hansk's R & D Department, including the operation of a R&D Centre in Hong Kong Science Park; a production factory in Hong Kong; and a R &D Laboratory in Keqiao Innovative Research Institute of Zhejiang University of Technology.
